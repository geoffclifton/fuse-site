About Fuse

Fuse is a messaging application designed with privacy and simplicity in mind. The app allows users to communicate directly with one another through private messages.

One-to-one conversations use end-to-end encryption so that only the sender and recipient can read the messages. Group chats may use standard server-mediated delivery.

Fuse includes safety tools such as user blocking and reporting to help maintain a safe communication environment.
